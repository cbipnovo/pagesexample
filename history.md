# The History of Pizza 2

## 1987 — The Beginning

Sal Moretti arrived in Copenhagen from Naples with little more than his father's pizza recipe and a burning ambition. He found a small vacant shop on Vesterbrogade, scrubbed it clean, installed a secondhand coal-fired oven, and opened the doors to what would become a neighbourhood institution.

The name "Pizza 2" came from a simple fact — Sal's shop was the second pizzeria on the street. The first one closed within a year. Pizza 2 stayed.

## 1990s — Building a Reputation

Word spread quickly. Sal's insistence on 48-hour cold-fermented dough and imported San Marzano tomatoes set him apart in a city where fast pizza was the norm. By 1994, Pizza 2 had lines out the door on Friday nights.

In 1997, Sal upgraded to a custom-built coal oven capable of reaching 800°F — the same oven still in use today.

## 2000s — The Next Generation

Sal's son Marco joined the kitchen in 2003 after training in Naples and Rome. He introduced the specialty menu — the Vodka Pie, the Grandma Slice, and the White Pie all date from this era.

In 2008, Pizza 2 won "Best Pizza in København" from Politiken's food section for the first time. They've won it three more times since.

## 2010s — Community & Growth

Rather than open a second location, the Moretti family doubled down on community. Friday night open-mic started in 2012. The annual Thanksgiving pie giveaway (200 free pies to local families) began in 2014.

Sofia Moretti, Sal's granddaughter, joined the team in 2018 — handling front of house on weekends while studying at Copenhagen Business School.

## 2020s — Tradition Meets Today

Through the pandemic, Pizza 2 pivoted to takeaway and kept every staff member employed. The neighbourhood responded — regulars organised a "buy a pie forward" campaign that funded over 500 free meals for healthcare workers.

Today, Pizza 2 remains a family operation. The dough is still hand-stretched, the sauce still made fresh every morning, and every pizza still comes out of that same coal-fired oven Sal installed in '97.
