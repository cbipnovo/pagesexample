Our Story

Three generations of pizza perfection.

From Naples to Copenhagen

Sal Moretti opened the doors to Pizza 2 in 1987 with nothing but a family recipe, a secondhand oven, and a dream. His father had run a small pizzeria in Naples, and Sal brought those traditions to Copenhagen — hand-stretched dough, sauce made from San Marzano tomatoes, and a refusal to cut corners.

Nearly four decades later, the recipe hasn't changed. Sal's son Marco now runs the kitchen, and his granddaughter Sofia handles the front of house on weekends. The oven's been upgraded (twice), but the dough still rests for 48 hours, the sauce is still made fresh every morning, and every pie is still stretched by hand.

What Makes Us Different

  • 48-hour cold-fermented dough for that perfect crisp-yet-foldable crust
  • Sauce made in-house daily from imported San Marzano tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella delivered every morning from a local Copenhagen dairy
  • Coal-fired oven reaching 800°F for authentic charred flavor
  • No freezers on premises — everything is made fresh, nothing reheated

Community

We sponsor the local little league team, host Friday night open-mic, and every Thanksgiving we give away 200 free pies to families in the neighborhood. This isn't just a pizza shop — it's a gathering place.
